Privacy Policy

Your privacy is non-negotiable. We believe in transparency, security, and data minimization.

1

Introduction

Your privacy is very important to us. It is FileSwapr's policy to respect your privacy regarding any information we may collect from you across our website and any other services we operate.

2

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information or data from the files you upload for conversion. All uploaded files are processed securely on our servers and are automatically deleted shortly after processing.

  • No data mining
  • No permanent storage of your files
  • No selling or sharing of content
3

How We Use Your Information

The content of your uploaded files is used solely for the purpose of providing the requested file conversion service. Once the conversion is complete, the files are scheduled for immediate automatic deletion from the temporary storage (`/tmp` directory).

4

Security

We take your data security seriously. All communication between your browser and our server is encrypted via SSL/TLS standards. Uploaded and converted files are stored strictly in temporary environments and never exposed publicly.

5

Cookies

FileSwapr utilizes essential cookies solely for website functionality. We do not use cookies for tracking, analytics, or targeted advertising purposes.

6

Third-Party Services & Links

We utilize trusted serverless infrastructure (Vercel). We do not share your file data with third parties. Our site may contain links to external sites; we are not responsible for their privacy practices.
